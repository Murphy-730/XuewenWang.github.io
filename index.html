About me

Wang Xuewen, born in Rizhao, Shandong Province in May 1994, Ph.D., lecturer. She graduated from the Applied Mathematics major of the Institute of Mathematics and Systems Science, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2023, and is currently a teacher of the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics.
